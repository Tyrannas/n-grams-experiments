The first bar of the intro/verse is the main picking pattern. Make sure to keep a consistent beat using your
thumb on the alternating bass notes while playing concurrent notes with a pinch motion. A good way to learn 
is to keep the same pattern as the first bar for all chords to start (which is generally correct throughout 
the song), then try some of the variations John introduces into the intro/verse and elsewhere.
Tabbed by watching a couple of live performances:
- Live in Boston (https://youtu.be/TGNrzQ8Jxvg)
- Live @ the Forum (https://youtu.be/W8r45SUQc14)
