Casey's Song
Dallas Green/City and Colour
Tabbed by Alex Re-Re

First let's start with the tuning:

- start with: E A D G B e
- press 4th fret on the E and tune with the A
- this turns A into Ab or G#
- press 3rd fret on A(G#)string to tune with the D
- this turns D into B
- press 7th fret on D(B)string to tune with the G
- this turns G into F# or Gb
- press 5th fret on G(F#)string to tune with the B
- they should sound the same
- press 4th fret on B string to tune with the e
- this turns e into eb or D#
- you are left with: E G# B F# B D#
- put capo on 2nd fret

h = harmonic
