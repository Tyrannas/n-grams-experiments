Done.
All in all, it's a pretty simple song -- No chords to remember, it's actually
just playing a blues scale a few frets down.  Practice it for a little while.
The hardest part is hitting the open 1st string on almost every note.  It's
a real pain.  If anyone can figure out the stuff he does during the chorus,
it would be greatly appreciated.  I think it's just playing muted and semi-muted
strings, but I'm not sure.

From Plastic Ono Band, 1970
