Band - Margot and the Nuclear So & So's
Song - Broad Ripple Is Burning
Album - Released Demo
Tabber: Jon Green

This is my finger picking version of the song Broad Ripple Is Burning by the
band Margot and the Nuclear So & So’s. It sounds ok when you play along to 
the song. You can either strum the chords or finger pick.  This is tabbed 
according to the demo version released on the internet. Its very simple and 
very repetitive. So I urge someone either to build off of this or send 
corrections through the comment section.
