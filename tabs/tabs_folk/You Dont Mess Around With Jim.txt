You Don't Mess Around With Jim
Jim Croce
metonymo tab

Standard tuning (tuned down half step, instead of E (eadgbe) it is D# (d#g#c#f#a#d#)).
The tab is from the "fretkillr" version (se youtube).
The shuffle rhythm is mostly like that in the last four bars of the intro:

  ^     ^  v   ^     ^  v   ^     ^  v   ^  v  ^
  E7/h         E7/c#        E7/d         E7/c#
  :  .  .  .   :  .  .  .   :  .  .  .   :  .  .  .
-----------------------------------------------------


h = hammer-on
p = pull off
x = muted (mostly with ring finger)
^ = downstroke
v = upstroke
