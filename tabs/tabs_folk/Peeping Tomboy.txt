Tuning GGDGBD

Use this chord and add pinky (for hammers and pulls on the 5th fret) for the first part 
of the verse.
D (E) 4
B (B) 3
G (G) 4
D (D) 0
G (A) 0
G (E) 0

Use index finger on whole fret for the rest of the song and add pinky (and ring finger
if you like) for the chorus.

Play the bassline with thumb throughout the whole song:
String 5 4 5 4 5 4 5 4 5 4 ...etc for the verse
String 6 5 6 5 6 5 6 5 6 5 ...etc for the chorus and bridge
