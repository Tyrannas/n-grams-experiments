[Order of Song]

1st half of the song:  Verse 1, Fill 1, Fill 2, Verse 2, Bridge, Chorus, Verse 1, Verse
2, Bridge, Chorus.
Solo 1: Guitar 2 plays its riff during the whole solo. After 1 time of Guitar 2fs riff,
Guitar 3 kicks in.
After 2 times of Guitar 2fs riff and after the first measure of Guitar 3, Guitar 1 kicks
in. Guitar 3 ends when Guitar 1 finishes its first 2 repeats, and moves on to the next
measures. When Guitar 2 finishes, it switches to distortion and plays the same riff 2
more times, and Guitar 1 plays another measure along with Guitar 2. Then, both guitars
lay the following fill and bridge.
Solo 2: Guitar 1 plays under Guitar 2 for the entire solo.
Interlude: Both guitars play the first measure. Guitar 1 plays the second measure 3
times.  Then, Guitar 2 joins and plays riff 3 & 4 with Guitar 1. Last fraction of song:
Verse 1, Verse 2, Bridge, Chorus.
Outro: Guitar 1 and 2 play the last riffs. Guitar 3 plays some of the first solo
backwards. The very last riff is played by Guitar 1 & 2 and let the last chord ring.
Guitar 3 kicks in again and plays the reversed solo again.
