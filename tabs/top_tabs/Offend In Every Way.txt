This is an awesome song off of the White Stripes album White Blood Cells. I wasn't seeing tabs 
for this song around so I decided to write one. This is my first tab, but I've had plenty of 
experience looking at and learning tabs. I'm 98% sure this is the right way to play it, but hey, 
mistakes happen. Oh, and on the 7h8 part in the intro, it sounds cooler if you bend down on 7. 
It pulls off the same sound. Listen to the track to get the right rythym and strum pattern. 
Ok, enough talk. Play.

Standard Tuning (E,A,D,G,B,E)
PM = Palm Mute
