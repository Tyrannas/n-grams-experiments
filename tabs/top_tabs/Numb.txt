-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tabbed by: Karleen Heong
Email: the_aurora_australis@hotmail.com

Tuning: Drop D down 1/2 a step
SO: 6th(lowest)=Db 5th=Ab 4th=Db 3rd=Gb 2nd=Bb 1st(highest)=Eb

This song is tabbed for either one guitar or two. For one, it's best to play electric 
with distortion pedal, if you want to play it acoustic, it's fine too.
For two guitars, play one clean (no distortion), the other with distortion.

Note: Seeing that Linkin Park is a 6 person band, this version tabbed
out is obviously a little different. I've put in a little bit of my
style in it with different strumming rhythms, just to make it a
little more interesting for live performances, not a full band with
all instruments.

If using a distortion pedal:
Level=1/2 tone=2/3 bottom=2/3 distortion=full gain boost=1/2


| ()  harmonic
| *  Palm mute
