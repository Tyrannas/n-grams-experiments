-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                             In The Long Run - The Staves
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I originally learned this tab from a video on youtube by Cams Campbell, but it 
never sounded quite the way I wanted it to... I ended up finding a video of 
The Staves doing a slightly different version from the recorded song and I liked 
it a lot better. Also in that video you can see Jessica playing her guitar in 
several shots and I used that to modify the way I play the song, and I think  
it's more accurate now.
Here are the videos for reference:
Cams Campbell: 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kX8zPWgzkUA
The Staves:
https://youtu.be/cQXNUGMERQM


Tuning: Standard
Capo 3

I play the G chords as 3,4,5 rather than 2,3,4 (with the exception of the bridge)
and pretty much keep my pinky finger on the last string for most of the song.
