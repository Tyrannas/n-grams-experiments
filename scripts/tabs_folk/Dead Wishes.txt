NOTE: Excluding the intro, every bass note is played on the beat, so it's up to you to figure out the timing 
of all the notes in the middle. I've tried to help by using spacing, but there's only so much I can do.

There are 3 verse sections (it plays exactly as written until about half way through the first verse) but
the rest of the song uses the same fingering so you just have to mix and match it.

I play chorus with chords (which can be found on other tabs), but I'm working on an alternate version with 
capo on the 3rd fret that includes the chorus finger picked too.

Video of me playing here if you need more help: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cvxlk9kmoKM&amp;lc
