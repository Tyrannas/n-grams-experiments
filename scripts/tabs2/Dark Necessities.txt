might help out any other guitarists that were hoping to do the same.

if you have a better idea please feel free to post corrections and stuff.

Happy learning from your fellow RHCP lovin' guitarist.

[Outro/Solo]

This whole section has a lot of tremolo effects on it, if you've got a delay
pedal just turn up the repeats a lot, I think it has a bit of the 'phase' 
effect as well, not too sure tho. 

If you don't have any pedals you could get away with just picking each note 
a few extra times.
