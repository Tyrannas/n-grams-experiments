***********************************
how to get the sound of the intro: 

* set your delay repeats to 4-5 
* set your delay time to follow the tempo (around 2-3 ms) 
* plug your delay pedal BEFORE your disortion pedal 
* put your distortion at the max 
* mute all the strings with your right hand 
* put your hand on the fretboard and loop this tablature fast: 

***********************************

| /   Slide up
| \   Slide down
| h   Hummer-on
| p   Pull-off
| +   Harmonic
| x   Dead note
| b   Bend
|(n)  Ghost note
| PM  Palm mute
| ~   Vibrato
