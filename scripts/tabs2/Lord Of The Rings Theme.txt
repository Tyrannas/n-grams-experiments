This is a classical guitar piece, so you must know how to pick with your fingers. Although I did
NOT write this tab, I did do some editing. The original tab was by a YouTuber, Tim Parsons.
Here is the video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e9gYID6Ij3o&amp;feature=related

NOTE: This piece is in the key of C. If you want to play a more accurate, impressive, difficult
version in the correct key of D, check out Harry Murrell's arrangement: https://youtu.be/EYUaW68Xdh0
(his tab is on his website, in the description). This is the version that I prefer to
play today. -Aidan
