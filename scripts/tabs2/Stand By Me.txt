Here is a fun way to play this classic that incorporates the guitar strumming along with the
bass line that goes throughout the song. It works great when playing an acoustic by yourself.


[Main Theme]

            C                            Am                     F

G|----------0-0--0-------0-0--0----------2-2--2-----2-2--2-------2-2--2-----|
D|----------2-2--2-------2-2--2----------2-2--2-----2-2--2-------3-3--3-----|
A|----2-3---3-3--3--2-3--3-3--3--3-2-0---0-0--0--0--0-0--0-0-----3-3--3---0-|

     G             C
e|---3-3-3---------0-0--0---------0-0--0--|
B|---0-0-0---------1-1--1---------1-1--1--|
G|---0-0-0---------0-0--0---------0-0--0--|

That progression repeats for the duration of the song. It's simple yet effective and a 
lot of fun to play/sing.
The chords can be strummed as shown, picked,
or both however you see fit. The timing is the main aspect.
Mix it up. Have fun with it...
