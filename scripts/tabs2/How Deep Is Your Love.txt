HOW DEEP IS YOUR LOVE
Originally performed by the Bee Gees


Extremely nice rendition here.  After working out all the chords I can definitely
John missin' the progression at one point after learning it that same day.  Keep in mind that
he plays most chords with his thumb wrapped around the neck of the guitar.
I believe this is as accurate as humanly possible. You can view the video here:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UODlieJdQT4
