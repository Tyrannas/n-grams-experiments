                           Innerspeaker by Tame Impala
                    Released in 2010, written by Kevin Parker

This is my favourite album of all-time, by my favourite band of all-time. All of these
tabs are my own work, and have been thoroughly checked for accuracy and completeness.
Every single sound on the album made by the guitars and synths (and even a bit of bass
too) is here. I made sure to include everything you hear on the album in this tab.

I've been working on this for a long, long time, so I hope you enjoy playing the songs
and be sure to support Tame Impala in any way possible. They more than deserve it!

Tracklist:

10. Runway, Houses, City, Clouds

the song. Now without further ado...
