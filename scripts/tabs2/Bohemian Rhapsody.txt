Bohemian Rhapsody - words and music by Freddie Mercury

Brian May's sound consists of a treble boost pedal, Vox AC30 and a Brian May Special guitar.

The Brian May guitar was an outstanding design, it allows you to put any pickups in phase or
out of phase. It was a great invention by him and his dad. Lots of Brian May's tone comes
from the guitar and let's not forget his fingers. The Vox AC30 is still on sale now but
a very clean sounding amp and in order to achieve that Brian May sound you're going to
have to push the amp and add a treble boost pedal.

Brian May's sound is very unique and trying to recreate his sound can take a very long time.
